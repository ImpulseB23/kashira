# code of conduct

be decent to each other. that's basically it.

## the longer version

- be respectful in discussions, reviews, and issues
- no harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- give constructive feedback, not destructive criticism
- assume good intentions
- if someone's being a problem, report it to the maintainers

## enforcement

maintainers can remove comments, close issues, or ban users who violate this. we'd rather not, so just be cool.

## contact

open an issue or reach out to the maintainers directly.
